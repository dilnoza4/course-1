What are Minions?

Minions are small, yellow, and funny creatures known for their love of bananas and their silly antics. They first appeared in the *Despicable Me* movie series and quickly became fan favorites!

Popular Minions

Some of the most popular Minions include Kevin, Stuart, Bob, and Dave. Each Minion has its unique personality and role within the group.

Fun Facts about Minions

Did you know that Minions speak their language called "Minionese," which is a mix of various languages and gibberish? Their love for bananas is a constant theme in their adventures!
